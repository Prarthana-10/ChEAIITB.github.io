ChEA BLOGS

Internship Blogs

It is rightly said that the 3rd year summer intern proves to be a career-defining one. Preparations for the same begin at the end of the 2nd year itself. We are grateful to our seniors, who have shared their preparation stories and experiences through numerous articles.

Placement Blogs

Every year, the final year students appear for the placements to begin a new phase of their lives after completing their education. Since it is the first time students appear for the placement season, they are bound to get nervous since placement is indeed a career-defining step

InternEra

Internera is a special blog series to introduce second-year undergraduates to the world of internships. The third-year B.Tech students have curated articles sharing their experiences of the 2nd-year winter and summer interns. The prime focus is on the apping process and its challenges

ICY Didn't Know

In Case You Didnt Know is a blog series by ChEA which highlights the various different opportunities for the chemical engineer of today. We had alums from a vast variety of fields and they with their rich and vast experience were quite insightful about these unique career avenues

Apping Guide

The Placement Cell brings in a plethora of internships in various sectors. However, at times one's interests and aspirations are not appropriately catered through them. In that case, one might have to search for an opportunity on ones own and that is when an apping guide comes handy!

Then Vs Now

Then Vs Now is an innovative blog series that highlights the stark contrast between 'then' and 'now'. This year we looked deeper into different fields and topics that have changed in the past few years including entrepreneurship, placements, gender diversity in the workplace, academia etc.

SemEx

IIT Bombay offers a unique opportunity to pursue a semester at a foreign university instead of doing so at IITB to get exposure outside India. Going through the program, a student learns from a completely different perspective and is also exposed to the work culture and opportunities abroad.
